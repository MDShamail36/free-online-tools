Word Counter

The Word Counter is an essential tool for writers, students, and bloggers. It calculates the number of words, characters, and estimated reading time for any text or document.

Features

  • Count words and characters accurately
  • Estimate reading time
  • Helps in writing assignments, essays, and articles
  • Fast and reliable online tool

How to Use

Copy and paste your text into the tool. It will automatically display word count, character count, and estimated reading duration.

Benefits

Perfect for bloggers, students, and professionals who need precise word counts for assignments, SEO content, or publication requirements.
