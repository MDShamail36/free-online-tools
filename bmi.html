BMI Calculator

The BMI Calculator helps you evaluate your body weight in relation to your height. It is a valuable tool for understanding whether your weight is healthy, underweight, or overweight.

Features

  • Input height and weight to calculate BMI
  • Instant results with health category
  • Guidance on fitness and weight management
  • Easy and user-friendly interface

Usage

Enter your height in centimeters and weight in kilograms, then click "Calculate". The tool will display your BMI and indicate your health status.

Benefits

This calculator is helpful for anyone looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle. It provides quick insight into your body health and assists in creating a fitness plan.
