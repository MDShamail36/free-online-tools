Text Case Converter

The Text Case Converter allows you to quickly change the case of any text. Whether you want uppercase, lowercase, or capitalized words, this tool makes it simple.

Features

  • Convert text to Uppercase, Lowercase, or Capitalize
  • Copy and paste functionality
  • Perfect for bloggers, students, and writers
  • Fast and easy to use

How to Use

Paste your text into the input box, choose the desired case option, and click "Convert". Your text will be updated instantly.

Benefits

This tool saves time when formatting text for blogs, essays, or professional documents. It ensures consistency and readability.
